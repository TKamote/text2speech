## General Tips for AI Image Prompts

### Key Elements to Include:
- **Subject**: What you want to show
- **Style**: Minimalist, technical, diagram, etc.
- **Color scheme**: Dark theme, purple accents
- **Text**: White, bold, readable
- **Background**: Dark gray (#1F2937) or transparent
- **Aesthetic**: Professional, modern, clean

### Example Structure:
```
[Subject] + [Layout/Arrangement] + [Style] + [Colors] + [Text Style] + [Background]
```
